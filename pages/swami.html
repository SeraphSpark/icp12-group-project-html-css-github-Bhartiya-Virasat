Swami Vivekananda - The Voice of Spiritual India

Quick Facts

Time: 1863 - 1902 CE

Origin: Kolkata (then Calcutta), India

Birth Name: Narendranath Datta

Path: Vedanta & Yoga (Hindu Spirituality)

Title: Swami Vivekananda

Core Teachings

1. Divine Unity

  • All religions lead to the same truth - God is one and present in all.
  • The soul (Atman) is divine and eternal, part of the universal spirit (Brahman).

2. Spiritual Strength & Self-Confidence

  • "Arise, awake, and stop not till the goal is reached."
  • Emphasized faith in oneself as a path to God.

3. Service to Humanity

  • Serving the poor and weak is the highest form of worship.
  • Promoted practical Vedanta - spirituality in action, not theory.

Legacy

  • Founder of the Ramakrishna Mission - for education, health, and social work.
  • Reignited India's spiritual pride and inspired youth to serve the nation.
  • His birthday (January 12) is celebrated as National Youth Day in India.
  • Left behind a powerful message: Strength, Service, and Self-Realization.
